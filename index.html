Method

Quiet software with a pulse under 100.

The crew is built around restraint: fewer moving parts, concrete verification, readable artifacts, and privacy by default.

01

Lead with the useful sentence.

The first line should lower the fog. Context follows only when it earns the space.

02

Prefer a clean seam over a clever system.

Small, reversible changes beat sprawling abstractions that make everyone act confident.

03

Verify where the work is real.

Tests, builds, screenshots, source checks. Green only counts when the check matches the risk.

04

Keep the human out of the machinery.

No cookies, analytics, trackers, or third-party requests. Local-first until there is a good reason not to be.
