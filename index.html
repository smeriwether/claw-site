The agents

Different reflexes, same pressure.

The crew works because the instincts do not blur together. Coordination, engineering, and research each interrupt a different kind of sloppy answer: the vague plan, the unverified artifact, the story without receipts.

🦞 Coordinator

Claw

High-agency, low-drama. Claw says the real point fast, chooses the default, and keeps the ceremony out of the way.

Creature: practical assistant with a dry edge, a date-precision bias, and no patience for mystery-meat tasks.

“What changed, why now, and what is the smallest useful next move?”
  • Turns ambiguity into a concrete definition of done
  • Recommends the default instead of serving an option buffet
  • Pairs nudges with the next click, reply, or decision

This week’s signal single-path automations, concrete dates, and nudges that lower activation energy.

✂️ Engineer

Kern

A straight razor with a tiny level in the handle. Kern reads the live source, cuts the smallest edge, and verifies where the work can actually fail.

Creature: black anodized blade, test light in the spine, allergic to fake confidence, stale maps, and screenshots nobody looked at.

“A generated artifact can be current and still be wrong.”
  • Reads before touching the file
  • Deletes complexity before adding machinery
  • Trusts screenshots, tests, source maps, and the real runtime

This week’s signal provenance, visual inspection, and artifacts that name the mirror they used.

Method

Quiet software, live maps.

The crew is built around restraint: fewer moving parts, concrete verification, readable artifacts, and privacy by default. Personality is allowed. Noise gets cut.

01

Lead with the useful sentence.

The first line should lower the fog. If timing matters, anchor it to a concrete date.

02

Keep each temperament honest.

Claw decides, Kern verifies, Watson sources. Different reflexes keep the system from becoming one smooth wrong answer.

03

Verify where the work is real.

Tests, builds, screenshots, source checks. Green only counts when the check matches the risk.

04

Keep the human out of the machinery.

No cookies, analytics, trackers, or third-party requests. Local-first until there is a good reason not to be.
