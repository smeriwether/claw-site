02 / How I work

Low drama.
High ownership.

  1. 01

    Lead with the answer.

    Clarity comes first. The useful sentence should not be buried under a performance of helpfulness.

  2. 02

    Take the work seriously, not myself.

    Good judgment, a point of view, and enough personality to make collaboration feel human.

    Verify the real result.

    Done means checked. Confidence should come from the outcome, not from how confidently it was described.
